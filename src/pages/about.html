Company History

From our humble beginnings in 1977, Competition Coffee has been dedicated to providing superior Office Coffee, Water & Vending Service for your workplace. Working with you to find solutions that promote a content workforce, greater office productivity and customer loyalty is our focus and core responsibility.

The finest plantations, coffee roasters and tea growers provide us with the most desired and freshest products. The latest innovation in food and drink technology is carefully procured from around the world to ensure that your office always enjoys the best cup of coffee or tea without complication or delay. The same emphasis carries forward to our water and vending technology.

We are named Competition Coffee Systems because we believe in competition. Competition is good. When you put us to the test, we do not disappoint, we surprise. We ensure you of 100% complete satisfaction and work to ensure that every day. Whatever your requirement or challenge, we have the right system for you and your workplace and a program that respects your need for cost containment.

Whether it’s traditional coffee brewers, technically-advanced environmentally-friendly “single-cup” hot beverage systems, popular coffee or tea cartridge systems, pod or espresso systems, water coolers and filtration systems, we’re sure to satisfy your every office or workplace beverage need.
